    MINING & INDUSTRIAL CHEMICALS

    EnviroGREEN’s mining & Industrial products range includes the following:

  • EnviroGREEN’s Food and Beverage range of products covers a wideeagents including Sodium Silicate, Sodium Carboxymethyl Cellulose (CMC) and Xanthates for flotation treatment
  • Cleaning Chemicals for underground mining workshops
  • Machining coolants and other machine shop cleaning chemicals
  • Cleaning Agents in Electrical maintenance

    • FOOD & BEVERAGE CLEANING PRODUCTS

    EnviroGREEN’s Food and Beverage range of products covers a wide number of applications such as in hotels, kitchens, canteens restaurants, meat processing plants, breweries, beverage manufacturers, dairies, milling plants, and other food processing plants. Our range is complimented by a range of dispensing units and colour coded brushware and equipment.

    COMMERCIAL CLEANING PRODUCTS AND SERVICES

    EnviroGREEN’s Commercial range of products and services cover a wide range from office maintenance to all public facilities such as hospitals, education facilities, healthcare facilities, offices, shopping malls, ablution areas, individual homes, housing complexes, showrooms to name a few typical areas .. EnviroGREEN provides a complete solution (chemicals, equipment, and cleaning services) for daily maintenance in all areas. Our products have high dilution ratios and that guarantee you a quality cost effective solution. EnviroGREEN does not only enhance your environment, but protects and maintains it n a hygienic manner. Please contact our team regarding your exact requirements

    PEST CONTROL

    EnviroGreen offers affordable pest control services to residential and commercial establishments in Zambia.Pest Control Services include control of mosquitoes, termites, ants, rodents, cockroaches, bedbugs, wasps, and other forms of pests. EnviroGreen also offers fumigation of bagged grain to control rain borers. These services are mainly provided to farmers, grain aders, millers and importers and exporters of grain. Please contact our team regarding your exact requirements
